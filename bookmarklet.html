Why Not Just "View Source"?

Browser's "View Source" shows the original HTML served by the server. The bookmarklet captures the live DOM — including any JavaScript-rendered content, dynamically inserted script variables, and runtime-generated elements. This gives HyperSnatch more data to work with, often catching download links that aren't in the original source.
